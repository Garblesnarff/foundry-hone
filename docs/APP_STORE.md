## Description

100% local. 100% offline. 100% yours.

Every blurry photo is raw ore. Foundry Hone smelts it into sharpness — upscaling images 2× or 4× with AI precision, then forging every face back to crisp, true detail. No cloud uploads. No subscriptions. No waiting on a stranger's server.

Drop a photo into the forge. Hone fires up Real-ESRGAN to double or quadruple the resolution, then turns its furnace on every face in frame — detecting, restoring, and blending facial detail with GFPGAN so your subjects look like themselves again, not like wax.

**Sharpen every image. Restore every face. All on your Mac.**
- 2× and 4× upscaling via Real-ESRGAN — real sharpness, not fake blur
- Automatic face detection with RetinaFace — every subject gets full restoration
- GFPGAN face restoration blends seamlessly with the upscaled image
- Blend slider: 0–100% face restoration strength — from subtle lift to full reconstruction
- Anime model included: sharp, vibrant results for illustration and animated art
- Before/after comparison slider — drag across the forge to inspect every detail
- Progress streamed in real time — watch the furnace work via live updates

**Four AI alloys. One offline forge.**
Choose your temper: RealESRGAN x2 for fast doubling, x4 for full enlargement, x4-anime for illustrated work, or the realesr-general model for complex real-world shots. Every model runs entirely in your machine — ONNX-optimized for Apple Silicon, no GPU required.

**Built for photo archivists, portrait photographers, and anyone who refuses to lose a memory.**
Old family photos that never had enough resolution. Scans from damaged negatives. Screenshots blown up for print. Foundry Hone is the forge for images that deserve better than pixel soup.

Export as JPEG or PNG. Your originals never leave your machine. No telemetry. No phone-home. No surprises.

The blurry ore goes in. The sharp gold comes out.
